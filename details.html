Treadmill

Details

The Horizon T202 Treadmill comes loaded with preset programs that make it easy to achieve your fitness goals. Equipped with a powerful 2.75 CHP motor, 0 to 12% incline range, 60" deck and speed control up to 12 MPH, this high performance model will take your workouts to the next level.

No. of Equipments in Fitlife Center : 10

Specification

  • Horizon T202 Treadmill features easy-to-use programs that allow you walk, run or jog towards to your fitness goals
  • 2.75 CHP drive motor delivers smooth, quiet performance
  • 20” x 60" (50.8 x 152.4 cm) tread belt
  • Speed Range: 0.5-12 MPH
  • Incline Range: 0-12%
  • 5 workout programs with 10 levels each
  • Variable response cushioning
  • 3 Red LED, 5” (12.7 cm) LCD display and AFG App (tablet)
  • Dedicated speed and incline keys, quick program keys
  • Built-in fan to keep you cool and comfortable
  • Bluetooth 4.0, USB charging port
  • Audio jacks and 3W speakers
  • Dimensions: 70" x 34" x 56" (178 x 86 x 142 cm)
  • Weight Capacity: 325 lbs (147.4 kg)
