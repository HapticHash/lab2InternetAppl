Leg Press

Details

Safe, compact and high weight capacity are but a few features of the Powertec Leg Press. Being one of the most compact leg press machines on the market, this machine offers 1000 lbs. weight capacity. This leg press also comes with dual safety catches on each side, a large foot plate and an adjustable back rest for different leg press positions. The P-LP is a must have for every home gym. New for 2019, the P-LP comes with Stainless steel weight horns, improved padding material, and additional carriage position setting.
