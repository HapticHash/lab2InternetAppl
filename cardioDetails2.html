Exercise Bikes

Details

The Inspire Fitness CB1 Air Bike is a great option for those who enjoy exercise bikes but are looking for a full-body workout option! Use the pedals and moving arms for a full body workout, or place your feet on the pegs and engage only your upper body muscles.

No. of Equipments in Fitlife Center : 10

Specification

  • Dual-Action design delivers a full body workout
  • Front foot pegs allows user to focus on Upper body workout
  • Belt-Driven fan resistance provides air cooling and a smooth feel. The faster you pedal, the greater the resistance
  • Oversized seat for maximum comfort
  • Seat adjustment knob provides easy seat height adjustment
  • Rear transport wheels allow user to quickly and easily re-position the bike
  • Steel constructed frame provides maximum stability in a residential setting
  • Easy-to-read LCD screen provides feedback to the user. (Time, Speed, Distance & Calories burned)
  • Dimensions: 43.9" L x 24.8" W x 45.7" H
  • User Capacity: 300 Lbs
